No Kneed Bread- requires dutch oven and approx 15 hours

Ingredients:
  • 3 cups of flour (with more for dusting)
  • 1/4 teaspoon of yeast
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons of salt
Directions
  1. Combine dry incredients
  2. Mix wet and dry ingredients. Dough should be sticky.
  3. Let rest in a plastic-wrap covered bowl for 12-18 hours
  4. Dust flour over the dough and work the flour in until the dough can be handled without sticking to your hands. Shape into a ball.
  5. Let rest for another 2 hours.
  6. Approximately an hour and a half (or however long your oven takes to reach 450 degrees) after agitating your dough, preheat your oven to 450 degrees F and place your dutch oven inside to preheat with the oven.
  7. At the end of the doughs resting period, dust the dutch oven with some flour to prevent the bread from getting stuck to the dutch oven (I learned that the hard way). Then plop the (rounded) dough into the pot, cover, and place in the oven for 30 mins.
  8. Take the lid off the dutch oven and let the bread brown for 10-15 minutes until it goes from blonde to golden brown.
